ThermoGo App (2020)

Jumpstart Virtual Startup Competition
Language: Javascript
Tools: Figma, Canva Design
Contributors: Prisha Anand, Lina Battikha, Divya Jain

View on DevPost

Overview

The app ThermoGo, allows business of any size to monitor and update the daily temperature of their employees. This allows regulation during the COVID-19 pandemic become fast and easily accesible.

Implementation

On this group project, I designed the UI interface for the app, utilizing both Figma and Canva design. Later I aided in transferring this desing into Javascript.

View Slides

I also created the design elements for a professional business pitch for our prototype, which included aspects such as the business model, revenue, and reviews from potential customers. On the final day of the hackathon, I preseneted the pitch to a panel of four judges along with my group members.
